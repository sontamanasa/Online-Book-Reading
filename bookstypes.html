Fiction:

       Fiction is a literary genre where authors craft imaginative narratives, often featuring invented characters and settings, to tell compelling stories.Through fiction, readers can explore complex emotions, moral dilemmas, and societal issues in a safe and immersive way,fostering empathy and understanding.



   
  • Science Fiction

  • Romantic

  • Adventure

  • Poetry

Non-Fiction:

       Non-fiction literature comprises works rooted in facts and reality, providing readers with insights into the world's diverse subjects, from history and science to biographies and self-help. They empower readers to engage with the truth, enabling them to better navigate the complexities of the world around them.



   
  • History

  • Biography

  • Journalism

  • Philosophy
